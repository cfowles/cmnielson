About

With over 15 years of distinguished experience navigating the world of American Western art, Caitlin Nielson brings expertise to every appraisal. As an accredited member of the prestigious Appraisers Association of America, Caitlin has cultivated a reputation for meticulous attention to detail and comprehensive knowledge of this distinctive artistic tradition.

Based in the inspiring landscape of Sun Valley, Idaho, Caitlin's practice seamlessly blends scholarly rigor with practical market insight. Her extensive professional network spans top-tier museums, renowned galleries, discerning private collectors, and major auction houses across the country.

Appraisal Services

Every appraisal Caitlin conducts adheres strictly to the Uniform Standards for Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP), ensuring each valuation meets current industry standards and provides clients with legally defensible documentation. This commitment to excellence has made her a trusted resource for clients nationwide seeking valuations.

Caitlin provides a tailored service, working closely with each client to determine the scope of the work needed, and delivers thorough, well-researched appraisal reports. Her expertise encompasses multiple appraisal purposes, including:

  • Estate Tax or Planning - Comprehensive valuations that satisfy IRS requirements and facilitate smooth estate transitions
  • Charitable Contributions or Donations - Precise documentation supporting tax deductions for art donations to qualified organizations
  • Insurance Coverage - Detailed assessments ensuring proper protection for valuable Western art investments

Whether evaluating a single piece or an extensive collection, Caitlin brings the same level of dedication and expertise to every project, helping clients make informed decisions about their Western art assets.
